PactBuilderDSL - Java

A new OS library to automate the body DSL generation

Join José Maciñeiras and Alejandro Pena, two experienced engineers from Sngular, as they talk about their new open source library PactBuilderDSL recently released.

  • Alejandro Pena is part of the QE team at Sngular, with a passion for software development and a strong focus on automation.

    • He has more than two years of experience helping big companies to implement contract testing at an enterprise level.
    • Alejandro often posts about lessons learned from his time consulting, you can find an entire series here
  • Jose Maciñeiras is the lead of the OS3 team at Sngular, which is responsible for the development of open source libraries.

    • He has over 15 years of experience in the software industry and is a recognized expert in contract testing.

PactBuilderDSL is a Java library that automates the generation of DSL for bodys in contract definition.

Do you struggle with writing the body validations for contract testing? If you're working with Java... I'm pretty sure you do.

It's pretty common to have huge methods with very similar code to generate them.

This is a need we have detected many times not only in the community but also with our clients.

If all this is familiar to you, and you're interested in learning more, join us for our talk on PactBuilderDSL.

In this talk, Alejandro and Jose will discuss the following topics:

  • The need for a library like PactBuilderDSL
  • The features of PactBuilderDSL
  • How to use PactBuilderDSL
