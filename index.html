Welcome to center for information about Pact

What is Pact?

The Pact family of frameworks provide support for Consumer Driven Contract testing.

You can find documention on Pact under the Documentation menu, as well as on the Github pages for the various Pact implementations. The Ruby Pact one has a lot of information in the wikis.
