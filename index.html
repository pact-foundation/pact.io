Welcome to center for information about Pact

What is Pact?

The Pact family of frameworks provide support for Consumer Driven Contract testing.

Documentation

You can find documention on Pact under the Documentation menu and in the FAQ, as well as on the Github pages for the various Pact implementations. The Ruby Pact one has a lot of information in the wikis.

In particular, read up on how Pact works and Getting started with Pact for a guide on how to get started.
